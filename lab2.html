What Are We?

Cereal Club is a group of like minded freshman who all share a love for cereal. We were founded by Octavien Han and Youssef Assad as a means to meet more freshman outside of classes. We provide all the cereal, bowls, spoons, and milk (2% of course), so there is minimal effort required to participate, just come to the GT Connector on thursdays at 11:12pm and have fun!

How Do I Join?

It's easy! All you have to do is show up to the GT Connector at 11:12pm on thursdays and you are officialy apart of our cereal movement! You can also join our group me page to stay updated with our events and what not.
